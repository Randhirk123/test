PURPOSE:

Conduct Exam is a robust online exam software which offers impeccable assessment solutions through which Institutes, Companies, Universities and other organizations can conduct examinations with ease. The software comes as an answer to the undying efforts of teachers/moderators working towards modernization/simplification of the examination process and offers an easy to integrate collection of software applications for conducting online exam, offline/lab based exam and center based exam. Apart from covering these sectors of academia, Conduct Exam software has proven to be highly successful in campus recruitment by reducing the workload (human resource and cost consumption) of the company officials which would otherwise require them to go through each and every sheet of the aspiring candidates.
